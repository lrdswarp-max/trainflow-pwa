# Product Guidelines: PWA Web App

## 1. Design Philosophy

### Overall Visual Direction
A technological, energetic, and reliable visual, designed to convey innovation and performance. The aesthetic is inspired by the precision of the Tesla brand and the clarity of Apple, combined with the vibrant energy of a modern gym. The central concept is to create a visual identity that is as revolutionary and efficient as the PWA itself.

### Content Density
Medium density. Focus on 1-3 main ideas per view, with extensive use of images, graphics, and visual elements for strong impact. Generous white space (20-25%) for a sophisticated and modern feel.

## 2. Color System

**Primary Colors**
- Background Color: `#1A1F3A` (Professional dark blue, conveys technology and confidence)
- Primary Color: `#00D9FF` (Vibrant neon Cyan, represents innovation and energy)
- Secondary Color: `#FF006E` (Bold neon Magenta, adds personality and highlight)
- Highlight Color: `#06D6A0` (Fitness green, symbolizes health, progress, and success)

**Text Colors**
- Dark Text (for light backgrounds): `#1D1D1F`
- Light Text (for dark backgrounds): `#F7F9FB`

## 3. Font Design

**Heading Fonts**
- Hedvig Letters Sans (Distinct sans-serif with personality)
- Chiron Hei HK (Sharp geometric sans-serif for a tech feel)

**Body Fonts**
- Quattrocento Sans (Classic, highly readable sans-serif)
- Hedvig Letters Sans (For typographic consistency)

## 4. Image Style
High-quality images with a futuristic and energetic aesthetic. Modern gyms with dramatic lighting, glowing tech devices in dark environments, and focused athletic movements. Vibrant neon accents and deep shadows for high contrast.

## 5. UX & Interaction Principles
- **Simplicity First:** Eliminate redundant elements. Every component must serve a defined functional purpose.
- **High Responsiveness:** Immediate feedback for every interaction.
- **Inclusive Design:** Accessibility as a core requirement (WCAG standards).
- **Motion-Guided Flow:** Functional animations to guide focus and provide context.
